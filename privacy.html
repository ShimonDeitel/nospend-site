Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how NoSpend (the "app") handles information. The short version: NoSpend is built to keep your data on your device. It collects essentially no personal data, requires no account to use, and contains no trackers, analytics SDKs, advertising, or other third-party software development kits.

In plain terms: NoSpend does not have servers that store your information, does not sell or share your data, does not show ads, and does not track you across apps or websites. There is no account, no sign-in, and no cloud sync. The days you log and the figures the app shows you stay on your device.

1. Who we are

NoSpend is provided by Shimon Deitel ("we", "us", or the "data controller"). For any privacy question or request, contact us at s0533495227@gmail.com.

2. Information we do not collect

NoSpend does not ask you to create an account, and it does not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, location, health data, or device sensors. The app requests no device permissions. We do not operate analytics, advertising, attribution, or crash-reporting services that would receive your personal information.

3. Information you enter, stored on your device

The only information NoSpend works with is the information you choose to enter: which calendar days you mark as no-spend days, which days you log as a slip, the optional cost and note you attach to a slip, and your appearance preference. This information is stored locally on your device. From it, the app derives your streaks, day counts, and an estimated money-saved figure. These derived figures are presented as honest estimates and are never claimed to reflect your actual bank balance.

4. On-device only, no account, no sync

NoSpend runs entirely on your device. It does not use iCloud, CloudKit, Sign in with Apple, accounts, or any cross-device synchronization. Everything you log lives in a local database on the single device where you entered it and is never uploaded to us or to any third party. Because there is no account and nothing is synced, your data is not transferred between your devices; if you set up a new device, your NoSpend history does not carry over automatically.

5. Purchases

NoSpend offers a single optional one-time Pro unlock for $0.99 ("nospend_pro_unlock"). Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store and StoreKit. We never see or store your payment card details. Your purchase status is verified on-device through Apple's systems, and you can restore it on the same Apple Account using the in-app "Restore Purchase" option. Apple's handling of your payment information is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

6. No tracking, no ads, no third parties

NoSpend contains no third-party advertising, no analytics SDKs, no social-media SDKs, and no cross-app or cross-site tracking technologies. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with anyone, because we do not collect it in the first place.

7. Your privacy rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation, including the right to access, correct, erase, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data, and the right to data portability. Because the data you enter stays on your device, you can exercise most of these rights directly: you may view, edit, or delete any logged day inside the app at any time, and deleting the app removes the local data entirely. If you would like to make a formal request or have questions about the lawful basis for processing, contact us at s0533495227@gmail.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

8. Your privacy rights (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act, as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act, gives you the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to access and delete it, the right to correct it, and the right to opt out of its sale or sharing. NoSpend does not sell or share your personal information, and we do not collect personal information on our servers. We will not discriminate against you for exercising any privacy right. To make a request, contact us at s0533495227@gmail.com.

9. Children's privacy

NoSpend is rated 4+ and is suitable for general audiences, but it is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). Because the app collects no personal information on our servers, it is consistent with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will address it.

10. Data security and retention

Your logged data resides on your device, protected by the device's own security. Because we do not hold your data on our own servers and the app does not sync to the cloud, we have no separate retention period; your data persists for as long as you keep it in the app and is removed when you delete it or uninstall the app.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date above and post the updated policy at this address. Material changes will be reflected here, and your continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
